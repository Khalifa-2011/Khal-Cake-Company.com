Sponge Cake Recipe

Ingredient

  • 5 large eggs
  • 70 grams sugar
  • 50 grams all-purpose flour

Baking Instuction

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 9 x 9 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, add the egg yolks and half of the sugar(35g). Whisk together until completely combined. Sift in the flour. Whisk together until a smooth paste forms.
  3. In a cake mixer , add egg and mix at highest speed with a wire whisk attachment until egg turn foamy. Add in the remaining 35 g of sugar. Continue mixing at highest speed until egg form stiff peaks.
  4. Add 1/3 of the egg mixture into the cake batter Gently fold with a spatula until no white streaks remain. Repeat with another 1/3. Repeat with remaining 1/3. Be careful to only mix until egg whites are just incorporated and to do so in a gentle folding motion using a spatula and not a mixer or whisk. If you mix too hard you will deflate the egg whites.
  5. Pour batter into prepared baking pan. Bake in oven for about 10 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cake cool completely before slicing and decorating.
